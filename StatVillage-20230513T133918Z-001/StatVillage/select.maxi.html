Selecting units from StatVillage

To select units from StatVillage, simply click on the squares corresponding to the units desired and then press the left button at the bottom of the page (you may have to scroll down to find the buttons). You may wish to adjust your screen size to see the entire village at once. Units are numbered using a block and unit-within-block system.
